About Me

I am a geochemist currently in my sixth year of PhD studies at Caltech working with Jess Adkins to study the modern and ancient global sulfur cycle. More broadly, I am interested in reconstructing past Earth surface processes and chemical fluxes using the geochemical study of sedimentary materials. Our understanding of how these processes have operated in the past informs our predictions of how the processes may respond to future changes to the Earth system (especially anthropogenic global warming).
